# Mind-Expanding Zo Ideas — Batch 2

These are complete app-style cookbook ideas. Each card follows the existing app idea shape: Category, What it does, How to build it on Zo, Monetization, and Difficulty.

### 051. Vacation Price Scout
**Category:** Personal tools
**What it does:** Plans a trip like a patient research assistant instead of a booking site with a commission problem. It compares hotels, motels, cabins, fees, location tradeoffs, cancellation windows, parking, pet rules, and weird gotchas from reviews, then builds a short ranked brief.
**How to build it on Zo:** Trip intake form → browser/web research across booking sites and maps → price snapshot JSON → review summarizer → comparison table in a private zo.space page → daily price-check automation until booking.
**Monetization:** Personal use. Could become a paid travel-planning packet for families, road-trippers, or remote workers.
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

### 052. Warranty Escape Hatch
**Category:** Personal tools
**What it does:** Figures out what to do when a product breaks: warranty status, return window, manufacturer contact, required proof, repair options, and the exact message to send. Less “where did I put that receipt,” more “here is the packet.”
**How to build it on Zo:** Receipt/photo upload → OCR and email search → warranty database → manufacturer support lookup → claim packet generator → reminder automation for follow-up dates.
**Monetization:** Personal use, consumer advocacy tool, or local repair-shop add-on.
**Difficulty:** Intermediate
